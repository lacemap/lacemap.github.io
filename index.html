Points of interest related to handmade lace

Musea, monuments, sculptures, specialized libraries, archives or whatever lace enthusiasts might want to add to itineraries. Please check the accuracy of the provided information before making concrete plans.

An interactive map, created by volunteers. To share on websites of lace guilds, teachers, artists and collectors. Last changed entries and to-do list.

Propose changes

One method: leave a message for the interactive map manager of the Dutch lace guild LOKK. Follow the link in the right column almost at the bottom of the bulletin board.

Another method is the github procedure. In short: go to the list of entries, click the pencil, make changes, confirm three times with a green button at the bottom of the page. A moderator applies the proposed changes or discusses them. No pencil? Sign on/in first. At some steps you may drop a note. Click the map to get coordinates for a new entry. The long description with screen shots.

Translations

A human translation is available in Dutch, but that stretches only to the text around the map. The entries may be in English, the local language or the language of the contributor. Browsers facilitate automated translations, most require a plugin for that purpose.
