Points of interest related to handmade lace

Musea, monuments or whatever lace enthusiasts might want to add to itineraries. Please check the accuracy of the information before making concrete plans. You may need an appointment.

An interactive map, created by volunteers. To share on websites of lace guilds, teachers, artists and collectors. Last changed entries and to-do list.

Propose changes

Please don't take this service for granted and help the moderator with maintenace.

One method: leave a message for the interactive map manager. The contact link sits almost at the bottom of the bulletin board of the LOKK, a national Dutch lace guild.

Another method is a GitHub procedure. In short: go to the list of entries, click the pencil, make changes, confirm three times with a green button at the bottom of the page. A moderator applies the proposed changes or discusses them. No pencil? Sign on/in first. At some steps you may drop a note. Help pages of GitHub have a long description with screen shots.

For both methods you may need to figure out the coordinates for a new entry.

Translations

A human translation is available in Dutch, but that stretches only to the text around the map. The entries may be in English, the local language or the language of the contributor. Browsers facilitate automated translations, most require a plugin for that purpose.
