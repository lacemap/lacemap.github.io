Points of interest related to handmade lace

A map moderated by volunteers. Last changes.

Propose changes

Either inform the LOKK map moderator with the Dutch/translated form, or use your github account to propose changes to the list of entries (to be used in the first step of this procedure).

A new item on the map requires

  • A name of the public point of interest.
  • A brick-and-mortar address of the museum, monument, sculpture or specialized library or archive.
  • Coordinates which you can find by zooming in on the map and clicking the intended structure.
  • Optional contact information should not be an email address as there is no protection against robots. A website is fine as wel as a short remark about opening hours.
